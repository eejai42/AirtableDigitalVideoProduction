There are 3 messages in this lexicon sent by 2 different actors who speak and 2 actors who can hear messages.

Programmer

Programmer can say

  1. "Hello" to World in the general channel.
  2. "Goodbye" to World in the general channel.

Programmer can hear

  1. "Wassup" from World.

World

World can say

  1. "Wassup" to Programmer in the general channel.

World can hear

  1. "Hello" from Programmer.
  2. "Goodbye" from Programmer.

World can also hear everything that Programmer hears
